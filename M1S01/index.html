Projects

Mother of Waters

Mother of Waters is a short interactive visual novel about a woman facing unexpected consequences of her most recent meal. The story takes place in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, and the supernatural elements are inspired by Brazilian folkloric legends. A single playthrough takes about 20 minutes and there are 5 different endings.

Made with Ren'Py.

Warning: this story contains a scene of sexual nature as well as (briefly) depicts graphic violence and some nudity. If you're not supposed to see those, then please don't.

Story and writing by Vicente Marinho.

All artwork, programming and interface by Felipe Quérette.
